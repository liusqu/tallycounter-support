FAQ

How do I count?

Tap the big + button, or use your iPhone's volume buttons for hands-free counting.

What are laps?

Laps let you start a new count within the same session — perfect for sets, rounds, or intervals.

Does Tally sync across devices?

Yes. Tally uses iCloud to sync your counters across all your devices automatically.

What data do you collect?

None. Zero. Your data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We never see it.
