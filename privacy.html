Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Overview

Tally — Simple Counter ("Tally", "we", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

Tally does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not use analytics, tracking, advertising SDKs, or any third-party services that collect user data.

iCloud Sync

Tally uses Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) to sync your counter data across your devices. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is governed by Apple's privacy policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Data Storage

All your counter data is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We never see it, never collect it, and never sell it.

Third-Party Services

Tally does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Tally does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at liusqu@gmail.com.
